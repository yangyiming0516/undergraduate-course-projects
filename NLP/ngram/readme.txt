init.cpp is for the initialization of the data. the results are saved in train-init.txt and test-init.txt. 
bigram-cnt/unigram-cnt are for counting. the results are saved in bigram-count.txt and unigram-count.txt
in bigram.cpp, there are add-del smoothing, good-turing smooth and add-del+backoff
in bigram-new.cpp, there is good-turing + katz backoff
the answer is saved in myanswer.txt and check.cpp is for comparing with the standard answer and calculate the rate of correctness.
